Crystal Collector

Good Luck

You will be given random number at the beginning of the game.

There are four crystals below. By cicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win by matching your total score to the number picked randomly by the computer. You lose if your score goes above the computer's number.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game starts the crystal gets new value.
